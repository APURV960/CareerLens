Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about how our AI pipelines find your matches?

How does the semantic matching algorithm calculate scores? ▼
Unlike basic matching that searches for exact words, we use Sentence Transformers to encode your resume and open roles into high-dimensional vectors, evaluating overall context. This is combined with specific weights for skill overlaps, title similarity, work settings, and experience.
Is my resume stored securely? ▼
Yes, all resumes are stored in your secure, isolated PostgreSQL schema. Files are mapped to your local session identifier on your machine, ensuring complete privacy.
Can I edit cover letters before applying? ▼
Absolutely. We provide an interactive inline editor on the results page. You can customize the cover letter body directly in the browser before copying it or downloading it as a text file.
